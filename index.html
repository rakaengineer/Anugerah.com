About the Airport

Frans Kaisiepo International Airport is the main airport serving Biak Island and the surrounding region. It's named after the national hero Frans Kaisiepo from Papua.

Facilities

The airport features modern passenger facilities including check-in counters, baggage claim, immigration services, and various amenities for travelers.

Connections

Regular flights connect Biak to major Indonesian cities, as well as some international destinations in the Pacific region.
